Snehal Salunkhe

Your Daily Yoga Mentor &Guide

Hi, I’m Snehal K, founder of Snehyoga and a government-certified Yoga Teacher &Evaluator (YCB Level 3). I’m also trained in Ayurvedic Nutrition, Face Yoga, and Pregnancy Yoga and I hold a World Record in Yoga. But my journey into yoga wasn’t just about achievements.

Years ago, I saw how stress, irregular routines, and unhealthy lifestyles were silently draining people - professionals, homemakers, students, and even seniors. I noticed that while many wanted to practice yoga, most quit because of two reasons: high costs and lack of regularity.

I knew yoga had the power to transform not just the body, but the mind, breath, and lifestyle. That realization pushed me to create something different, something affordable, consistent, and accessible to everyone.

It wasn’t easy. I had to step beyond traditional teaching models, experiment with online classes, and build trust in a space where people were used to expensive studios or app-based routines. But I kept going, because my mission was clear: to make yoga a daily lifestyle, not just an occasional practice.Today, I’ve trained over 10,000+ students across my programs and built a thriving community of people who wake up every day to a healthier, calmer, and more energized version of themselves.My story isn’t just about yoga asanas. It’s about discipline, inner peace, and balance values I now share with you through the Daily Yoga Program.👉 And now, I’m here to help you build a life of energy, focus, and inner calm-one daily yoga session at a time.
